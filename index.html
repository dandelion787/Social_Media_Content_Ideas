8 categories · ongoing posting reference

These are the 8 recurring content categories we can draw from every week. This will serve as the master reference for what to post, how often and in what format. The 10 planned posts are built from these buckets.

📊
1. Data & Market Stats
2× / week

Lead with a bold number that stops the scroll, then give it local context. UAE and MENA-specific figures land harder than global averages as the audience recognises their market.

Post Ideas:
  • UAE digital ad market hits AED 9.7B — what it means for small brands?
  • Average Google Ads CPC in Dubai vs the global benchmark
  • Carousels Get 3.1× More Engagement — The Proof
  • 68% of online experiences start with search: the SEO case in one stat
  • Instagram penetration in UAE: 80.1% (which is the world's highest)
  • How much Dubai businesses spend on digital marketing annually?
Best formats: Single Image Carousel
🎓
2. Education & How-Tos
3× / week

Teach the audience something they can act on today. Break down SEO concepts, explain how Meta's algorithm works, or demystify Google Ads.

Post Ideas:
  • Why your Google Ads cost more in Dubai and how to fix it
  • How the Meta algorithm decides who sees your ads in 2026
  • SEO basics for Dubai businesses
  • 74% of Gen Z Discovers Brands on Instagram — Is Yours Optimised for Search?
  • Arabic vs English keywords: when to use each in UAE campaigns
Best formats: Carousel Reel
🏆
3. Results & Case Studies
1–2× / week

Real numbers from real campaigns. Show the before and after: CPL dropped, ROAS improved, a brand climbed from page 7 to page 1.

Post Ideas:
  • CPL reduced from AED 80 to AED 30 — how we did it
  • Dubai F&B brand: from AED 5K to AED 30K monthly ROAS
  • Real estate client ranked Page 1 for 3 keywords in 90 days
  • Monthly performance dashboard — what our clients see every week
Best formats: Carousel
🔧
4. Top Tools & Resources
1× / week

Recommend free SEO tools, landing page builders that support Arabic, or Google Ads scripts that save hours.

Post Ideas:
  • 5 free SEO tools every Dubai business should bookmark
  • Best landing page builders that support Arabic + English layouts
  • Google Ads scripts that save 3 hours of manual work per week
  • Tools we use internally at Traction Studios (our actual stack)
  • Free creative testing tools for Meta Ads — ranked
  • Top keyword research tools for UAE and Gulf markets
Best formats: Carousel Single Image
🧠
5. Thought Leadership & Hot Takes
2× / week

Share opinions, not just information. Call out bad practices and make bold predictions about digital marketing. Aim: spark debates to earn more reach.

Post Ideas:
  • Why chasing followers is the wrong Instagram metric in 2026
  • The biggest mistake Dubai brands make when running Meta Ads
  • Most UAE brands don't need more traffic — they need better conversion
  • Prediction: where MENA digital marketing is heading in the next 2 years
Best formats: Reel Single Image
😄
6. Relatable Humor & Memes
2× / week

Lean into everyday agency-life frustrations to use humour as a way to connect with Dubai marketers.

Post Ideas:
  • "Go viral. Budget: AED 500. Timeline: by Friday." — the meme post
  • Explaining SEO to a client who just wants to rank #1 by tomorrow
  • Client feedback: "It's good but can you make it more… creative?"
  • Agency life: when the campaign finally goes live and you refresh every 30 seconds
  • The difference between what clients ask for vs. what they actually need
  • Monday morning energy at a digital marketing agency
Best formats: Single Image Reel

2× / week

BTS content to humanise Traction Studios and makes it feel real. Keep it authentic to build trust.

Post 02
🎓 Education
7-Slide Carousel
"Why Your Google Ads Cost More in Dubai (And How to Fix It)"
Research Basis
Dubai CPCs range AED 8–45 in real estate and AED 20+ in accounting. Core reasons: mixing English and Arabic in one campaign destroys relevance score; broad match keywords waste budget; ignoring Quality Score directly inflates CPC. Separate campaigns per language are required for efficiency in the UAE.
Why It Will Perform
Carousels hold a 0.55% engagement rate — the highest format in 2025. "Mistakes" posts earn the most saves because people return to them. Speaks directly to business owners spending money on ads right now.
Slide Breakdown7 slides
  • 01Hook: "Running Google Ads in Dubai? You're probably overpaying." — set the pain point immediately
  • 02Mistake 1: Mixing English + Arabic in one campaign — destroys relevance score
  • 03Mistake 2: Broad keywords — "digital marketing Dubai" wastes budget vs. specific intent terms
  • 04Mistake 3: No negative keyword list — paying for irrelevant research traffic
  • 05Mistake 4: Low Quality Score on landing page — Google automatically charges more
  • 06The fix for each mistake — one clear action per panel
  • 07Closer: "We audited 20+ Dubai accounts. This showed up every time." + CTA
💬 "Save this before your next campaign goes live."

7-Slide Carousel
"From AED 80 CPL to AED 30 — How We Cut a Dubai Brand's Lead Cost by 60%"
